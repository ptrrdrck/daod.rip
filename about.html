Welcome! Dao Drip 💧 was made to assist with casual study 🔎 of translations of the Daodejing 📜 (🗣 "Dow Duh Jeang").

The "drip" buttons select a random 🎲 chapter (out of 81) for you to ponder 💭 and place translations of the chapter together 🔺.

The differences and similarities reveal the translator(s) voice 🖍 and the nuance of the original underlying source 🕳.

Keep reading with the "drip" action, "view" chapter selector, and unread chapter links. Navigate your reading history, toggle translations and pick a theme. Your selections and history are saved locally in your browser.

Please leave some feedback if you find any issues, have a request or want to track updates.
