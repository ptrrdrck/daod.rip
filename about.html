Welcome! Dao Drip 💧 was made to assist with casual study 🔎 of translations of the Dàodéjīng 📜 (🗣 "Dow Duh Jeang").

The "drip" buttons select a random 🎲 chapter (out of 81) for you to ponder 💭. Your selected translations of the chapter are placed together 🔺. Their differences and similarities reveal the translator(s) voice 🖍 and the nuance of the original underlying source 🕳.

Your reading history, translation selections and theme are saved locally in your browser. Please leave some feedback if you find any issues, have a request or want to track updates.
